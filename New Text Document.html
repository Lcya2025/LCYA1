About Me

Hi, I'm Henry

With over 10 years of experience in wedding photography, I specialize in capturing the genuine emotions and beautiful moments that make your day unique. My approach combines artistic vision with documentary-style photography to create images that tell your story authentically.

Based in London but available worldwide, I've had the privilege of photographing weddings across Europe, Asia, and North America. Each wedding is different, and I take pride in adapting my style to match your personality and wedding vision.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me traveling with my family or experimenting with new photography techniques. I'd love to hear about your wedding plans and how I can help preserve your special day.
